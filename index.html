Academics

Strong classes, supportive teachers, and opportunities that help students prepare for college and beyond.

Patrick Henry High School offers a strong academic program designed to challenge students and prepare them for college, careers, and future success. Our campus provides a wide range of core classes, honors courses, Advanced Placement opportunities, and support programs that help students reach their goals.

Teachers at Patrick Henry are committed to creating engaging classrooms where students can build critical thinking, communication, and problem-solving skills. We encourage academic growth through personalized instruction, tutoring support, and access to resources that help every learner succeed.

Beyond the classroom, students can explore electives, career pathways, and enrichment programs that expand their interests and prepare them for life after high school. At Patrick Henry, academics are about more than grades — they are about building confident, capable, and motivated learners.

Athletics

Compete with pride, build teamwork, and grow through every sport and season.

Patrick Henry High School athletics play an important role in school spirit, teamwork, and student development. Our athletic programs give students the chance to compete, grow, and represent the Patriots with pride in a variety of sports throughout the year.

Coaches and staff work hard to build strong teams that emphasize discipline, dedication, leadership, and respect. Whether students are participating in varsity, junior varsity, or freshman sports, they are encouraged to push themselves and support one another both on and off the field.

From game days to training sessions, athletics at Patrick Henry create lasting memories and lifelong skills. Our programs help students stay active, build confidence, and become part of a community that celebrates hard work and Patriot pride.

About Us

A proud tradition, a strong community, and a commitment to helping students succeed.

Patrick Henry High School was founded in 1972 in the Del Cerro neighborhood of San Diego, California. Named after the great American patriot and Founding Father Patrick Henry, the school was established to serve the rapidly growing communities in eastern San Diego. From its humble beginnings with just a few hundred students, PHHS has evolved into one of the most respected comprehensive high schools in the San Diego Unified School District.

Throughout its rich history, Patrick Henry has consistently upheld high academic standards while building championship athletic programs and nationally recognized arts departments. The school’s green, gold, and white colors along with its Patriot mascot represent the core values of courage, integrity, and leadership that have guided generations of students. Many alumni have gone on to attend prestigious universities including UCLA, UC Berkeley, USC, and Ivy League institutions.

Today, with over 2,400 students, Patrick Henry High School remains deeply committed to providing a well-rounded education that prepares young people for success in college and beyond. The school continues to foster a strong sense of community, academic excellence, and school pride that has defined its legacy for more than five decades.
