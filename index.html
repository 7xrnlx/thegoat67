About Our School

Patrick Henry High School, located in the heart of San Diego, is a vibrant community of over 2,500 students. Named after the great American patriot Patrick Henry, we embody the spirit of liberty, excellence, and dedication to education.

Our beautiful 57-acre campus features state-of-the-art facilities including advanced science labs, performing arts theater, and athletic fields. With a proud tradition since 1968, we continue to rank among the top schools in the nation.

Colors: Forest Green, Athletic Gold, and White • Mascot: Patriots
